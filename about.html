History

I have had the pleasure of traveling with both my family and school friends, and each experience was unique in its own way. One of my most cherished memories is a road trip that I took with my family when I was younger. We traveled through several states, visiting national parks and historic landmarks along the way. Some of the places I have visited include; Nyahururu, Nairobi, Nakuru, Naivasha and Machakos.
